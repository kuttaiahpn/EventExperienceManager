Large-Scale Venue: Operations & Guest Logistics FAQ
Arrival & Entry Management

Which gate should I use for entry?
The venue features multiple dedicated gates (Gates A–G) based on your ticket type or zone.
Please check your digital ticket for your assigned gate; your barcode will only be valid at that
specific entrance.

How early should I arrive?
For large events, we recommend arriving at least 45–60 minutes prior to the start time to clear
security and find your zone comfortably.

Are there separate queues for different attendees?
Yes, we maintain separate lanes for VIPs, exhibitors, and general admission to ensure a smooth
flow. Look for high-mounted signage as you approach.

Entry, Re-Entry & Safety

What is the Re-Entry Policy?
Most large-scale events enforce a "No Re-Entry" policy; once your ticket is scanned and you
exit, you cannot return on the same ticket.

What should I do if I lose an item or get separated from my group?
o
Lost & Found: Head to the main Guest Services hub located at the Central Plaza.
o
Lost Children: Immediately alert the nearest security guard. We have a dedicated Child
Reunification Point at Gate D.

Is smoking or vaping permitted?
Our venue is a tobacco-free zone. Smoking and vaping are strictly prohibited inside the seating
bowls and concourses; designated smoking areas are located outside the main perimeter gates
only.
Digital Ticketing

Can I enter with a screenshot of my ticket?
No. For security, most modern venues require the live ticket barcode within the official app.
Screenshots often fail to scan correctly due to anti-fraud "refreshing" barcodes.
